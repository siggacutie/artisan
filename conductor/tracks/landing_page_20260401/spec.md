# Specification: Artisan Landing Page (Phase 1 MVP)

## Overview
Build the landing page for Artisan, a dark-themed, mobile-first web store exclusively for Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB). The landing page should be visually striking, conversion-optimized, and follow the design guidelines in `product-guidelines.md`.

## Features
- **Navbar:** Logo left, "Top Up" and "Accounts" links center, "Login" and "Sign Up" buttons right. Mobile-optimized with a hamburger menu.
- **Hero Section:** Full-width MLBB background art with a strong headline and two CTAs ("Top Up Now" and "Browse Accounts").
- **Trust Bar:** Displays three key badges: "Instant Delivery", "Secure & Safe", and "Worldwide".
- **Diamond Packages Preview:** A horizontal scroll row of 4-5 package cards showing diamond icons, amounts, prices, and savings.
- **Account Listings Preview:** A grid of three account cards showing rank, hero count, skin count, and price.
- **How It Works:** A simple 3-step guide with icons (Enter UID, Choose Package, Instant Delivery).
- **Testimonials:** Review cards with star ratings, names, and country flags.
- **Footer:** Logo, tagline, policy links, and payment method icons.

## Design Details
- **Theme:** Dark mode primary (#0a0f1e or near-black #0d0d0d).
- **Accents:** Gold (#ffd700) for primary CTAs and highlights.
- **Typography:** Rajdhani/Orbitron for headings, Inter for body.
- **Mobile First:** Ensure a seamless experience on a 375px wide screen with a bottom tab bar for navigation.
