# Product Guidelines: Artisan

## Branding & Visual Identity
- **Brand Personality:** Artisan should feel premium, trustworthy, and exclusive. It is the "specialist" store for MLBB.
- **Color Palette:**
    - **Background:** Deep Navy (`#0a0f1e`) or Near-Black (`#0d0d0d`).
    - **Primary Accent:** Gold (`#ffd700`) for CTAs, headings, and important highlights.
    - **Secondary Accent:** Electric Blue (`#00c3ff`) for secondary actions or glows.
- **Typography:**
    - **Headings:** Rajdhani or Orbitron for a bold, gaming feel.
    - **Body:** Inter for high readability and a clean look.
- **Iconography:** Use sharp, consistent line icons (e.g., Lucide React). Trust badges (shields, lightning bolts) should be used strategically.

## UX & Interaction Principles
- **Mobile-First Design:** All features must be fully functional and aesthetically pleasing on a 375px wide screen.
- **Speed & Efficiency:** Minimize clicks to checkout. Use one-click buy for wallet users.
- **Visual Feedback:** Every interaction (hover, click, loading) must have clear visual feedback. Use skeleton loaders for async content and smooth 300ms transitions.
- **Consistency:** Maintain a card-based layout across the site. Cards should have subtle borders and soft glows on hover.

## Content & Prose Style
- **Tone:** Professional, direct, and authoritative within the MLBB community.
- **Terminology:** Use standard MLBB terms (e.g., "Top-up", "Diamonds", "UID", "Server", "Skins").
- **Clarity:** Ensure pricing and savings are always clear. Show savings in currency amounts (e.g., "Save ₹18") rather than percentages.

## Accessibility & Performance
- **Contrast:** Ensure high contrast between text and background, especially for gold-on-black elements.
- **Performance:** Aim for sub-second page loads. Optimize all MLBB artwork and assets.
- **Touch Targets:** Minimum 44x44px for all interactive elements on mobile.
